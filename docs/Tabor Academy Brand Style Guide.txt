Tabor Academy Brand Style Guide
===============================

🧱 BRAND IDENTITY

Tabor Academy is a modern, interactive e-learning platform designed to rapidly equip individuals—especially from Africa's emerging markets—with entrepreneurial, digital, and freelancing skills through outcome-driven, project-based learning. Focusing on high-demand fields like digital marketing, no-code app development, e-commerce, AI tools, civil engineering solutions, and financial literacy, the academy combines microlearning, AI-powered and human-led personalized learning paths, mentorship and robust community support to help learners launch real-world businesses quickly. Its Africa-centric, mobile-first, offline-ready, gamified, and scalable design makes it an ideal gateway for aspiring entrepreneurs to move from learning to execution with confidence and tailored support.

This brand represents transformation through knowledge — where education fuels innovation, and learning leads to leadership.

🎨 BRAND COLORS & USAGE
------------------------

Primary Color:
- Orange (#FF6B35)
- Usage: Main call-to-action buttons, energetic highlights, progress indicators
- Emotion: Boldness, action, growth, learning momentum

Accent Color:
- Teal (#4ECDC4)
- Usage: Module highlights, icons, skill badges, links
- Emotion: Intelligence, clarity, trust, calm focus

Supporting Colors:
- Charcoal Gray (#2C3E50) → Main headings, content text
- Deep Forest Green (#1B4D3E) → Success states, highlighted notes, reinforcement areas
- Light Gray (#E5E8E8) → Card or course block backgrounds
- Soft White (#F7F9F9) + White (#FFFFFF) → Backgrounds, reading surfaces, clean space

🔤 TYPOGRAPHY
--------------
- Sans-serif font system for clarity (Inter, Poppins, Helvetica Neue)
- Headings: Bold, structured, intentional
- Body Text: Regular or Light, high readability
- Maintain consistent font sizing across all lesson formats and dashboards

🧭 LOGO USAGE
--------------
- Use full-color version on light backgrounds
- Use white version on dark backgrounds
- “Tabor” in Orange, “Academy” in Teal
- Always maintain clear space around the logo

📱 CROSS-PLATFORM DESIGN GUIDELINES
------------------------------------

Website:
- Use orange for CTAs, teal for highlights, white/gray for layout
- Maintain structured sections with clear headers

Mobile App:
- Orange for action buttons, teal for icons
- Keep interface clean and intuitive

Social Media:
- Bold text-based posts using brand mottos
- Orange + Teal background variations
- Maintain visual consistency in layouts

Print / Banners:
- Use orange as dominant brand color
- Support with teal in layouts and accents

Email:
- Branded header/footer
- Orange CTA buttons
- Teal icons or section dividers

Tabor Academy:
- Emphasize teal to reflect learning and intelligence
- Use orange to show action and momentum
- Stick to modern, minimal layouts

📚 ACADEMY-SPECIFIC DESIGN USAGE
---------------------------------

LMS Platform:
- Orange CTAs (Start Learning, Complete Lesson, etc.)
- Teal highlights for quiz status, course tags, skill areas
- Icons and badges consistently styled in teal with simple outlines

Certifications:
- Use deep forest green for approval messages and certification completion
- Orange headers for course titles and training levels
